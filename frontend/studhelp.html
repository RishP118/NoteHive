Getting Started

Welcome to NoteHive Student Dashboard. Here you can upload your notes, search and download shared materials, and join collaborative study sessions.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register and log in?

Click Sign In on the top-right of the dashboard. Enter your username and password, then verify your email if prompted. Once logged in, your session will stay active until you log out.

How can I upload my notes?

On the main dashboard, scroll to the Upload Notes section. Choose your file(s), enter a title and description, and select the appropriate subject. Then click Upload to save it. Supported file types: PDF, PPTX, DOCX.

How do I search and download notes?

Use the search bar at the top or the Search Notes section. Enter keywords or subjects, then click on any result to view or download the note.

How do study sessions work?

Go to the Study Session page from the dashboard. You can create a new session or join one using a session code. During a session, students can chat, share notes, and collaborate in real time.

How do I log out?

Click the Logout button on the top or bottom of the dashboard. This will securely end your session and return you to the login state.

How can I get help or report an issue?

Scroll down to the Contact Support section below and send an email to support@notehive.example describing your issue or feedback.
