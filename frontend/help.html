Getting Started

NoteHive helps you upload, edit, schedule and share notes with your students in a clean, professional interface.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload notes?

On the dashboard, open Upload Notes. Choose your files, add a title, select subject and description, then click Upload. Supported: PDF, PPTX, DOCX.

Where is the editor?

Click Open Editor from the Note Editor section or open editor.html directly.

How do I schedule a class?

Use the dedicated Scheduler page to add title, date & time, then hit Schedule. Invites are sent automatically (demo).

How do study sessions work?

Create a session to generate a code and share it. Students join using the code. End session to autosave notes (demo).

Can I restore drafts?

Yes. The editor autosaves to your browser storage. Open the editor again to continue where you left off.
